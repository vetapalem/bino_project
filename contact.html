How This Form Works

This form uses the mailto: method. When you click "Send Message", your default email client (like Gmail, Outlook, or Apple Mail) will open with all your form details pre-filled. You'll need to manually send the email from your email client.

Note: After clicking the button, your email application will open with a pre-filled message. You must click "Send" in your email client to actually send the message.

How It Works

  • Method Used: HTML mailto: protocol
  • No Backend Required: Works entirely in the browser
  • Requirements: User must have an email client configured
  • Data Format: Form data is added to email body
  • Customization: You can change the recipient email in the JavaScript

Limitations

  • Requires user to have an email client set up
  • Less reliable than server-side forms
  • No confirmation page after sending
  • Can't handle file attachments easily
  • Email formatting is basic
